About Bits & Bugs

Bits & Bugs is a family-owned Australian business established in 1999. Based on the Gold Coast, Queensland, Australia, our sales, marketing, and design operations are centered here, while our framed butterflies and insects are produced by our company in the Philippines. Each specimen is meticulously mounted by skilled artisans to highlight their vibrant colors and unique characteristics. We exclusively use shadowbox frames and ensure top-quality mounting, never compromising with cheap materials or substandard specimens. Our commitment includes dedicated customer support, just a phone call away!
